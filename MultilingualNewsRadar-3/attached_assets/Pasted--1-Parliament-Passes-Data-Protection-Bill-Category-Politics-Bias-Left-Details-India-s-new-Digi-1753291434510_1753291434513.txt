 1. Parliament Passes Data Protection Bill
Category: Politics | Bias: Left
Details:
India’s new Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Bill requires companies and government entities to seek explicit consent before collecting or processing personal data. It introduces the Data Protection Board of India, responsible for investigating violations and levying penalties. Critics argue that some government exemptions undermine the bill’s effectiveness, but digital rights groups still consider it a step forward.
