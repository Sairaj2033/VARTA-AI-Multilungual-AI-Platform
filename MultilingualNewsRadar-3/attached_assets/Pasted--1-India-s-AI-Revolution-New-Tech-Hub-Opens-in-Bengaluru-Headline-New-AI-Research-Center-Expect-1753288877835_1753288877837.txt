📰 2. Healthcare Goes Digital: India Launches National Telemedicine Grid
Headline: Government Connects 10,000 Hospitals Through AI-Powered Grid
Content:
To bridge the healthcare divide in rural India, the Health Ministry has launched a national telemedicine grid connecting over 10,000 public hospitals. The grid uses AI to assist diagnosis and automate appointment systems. “This ensures rural patients can consult top urban doctors,” said the Health Secretary. While critics warn of digital literacy gaps, the initiative is being hailed as a healthcare game changer.

📰 4. India to Regulate Generative AI
Headline: Draft Bill Proposes Registration for AI Models and Bias Audits
Content:
Concerned with misinformation, the Indian government is drafting a bill to regulate generative AI. The law mandates developers to register large language models and conduct fairness audits. Proponents applaud the ethical approach; critics fear it may hinder startups. “AI must evolve responsibly,” said an IT ministry spokesperson.
